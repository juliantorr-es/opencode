Record a prepublication review verdict of 'blocked' for a candidate boundary.
Writes a single JSON record under docs/json/opencode/approvals/prepublication_blocked.v1.json.
Each record captures schema_version, candidate_packet_digest, candidate_boundary_identifier,
parsed reviewer_set, review_round, status, blocker_description, repair_path, session_id,
and an ISO timestamp.

Output format:
  {"status":"blocked","candidate_boundary_identifier":"...","review_round":...}
