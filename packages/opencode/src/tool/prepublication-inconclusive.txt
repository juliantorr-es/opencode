Record a prepublication review verdict of 'inconclusive' for a candidate boundary.
Writes a single JSON record under docs/json/opencode/approvals/prepublication_inconclusive.v1.json.
Each record captures schema_version, candidate_packet_digest, candidate_boundary_identifier,
parsed reviewer_set, review_round, status, reason, session_id, and an ISO timestamp.

Output format:
  {"status":"inconclusive","candidate_boundary_identifier":"...","review_round":...}
