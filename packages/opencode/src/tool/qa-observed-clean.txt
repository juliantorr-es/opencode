Record a QA observation that tests exercise the production boundary cleanly.
QA observations are appended as JSONL under docs/json/opencode/sessions/<sessionID>/qa/observations.v1.jsonl.
Each record captures schema_version, plan_id, boundary, verdict, parsed tests_examined and
production_paths_exercised arrays, notes, session_id, and an ISO timestamp.

Output format:
  {"status":"ok","plan_id":"...","boundary":"...","verdict":"qa_observed_clean"}
